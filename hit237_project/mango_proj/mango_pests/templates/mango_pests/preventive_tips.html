Prevention Tips for Mango Pests & Diseases

General Mango Care Tips

  • Water trees regularly but avoid overwatering.
  • Apply mulch to retain soil moisture and control weeds.
  • Fertilize at the beginning of the growing season.

Sanitation & Orchard Hygiene

  • Clear fallen fruits and leaves weekly.
  • Disinfect tools after pruning infected branches.
  • Remove weeds that may harbor pests.

Pruning & Spacing

  • Prune during the dry season to reduce fungal spread.
  • Maintain at least 5 meters between trees for airflow.
  • Remove dense inner branches to allow sunlight in.

Biological Control

  • Use neem oil as a natural pesticide.
  • Introduce predatory insects like ladybugs to eat aphids.
  • Install pheromone traps to monitor pest activity.

Pest-Specific Prevention

  • Mango Hopper: Avoid over-irrigation, apply insecticides during flowering.
  • Fruit Fly: Use bait traps, remove infested fruits.
  • Mango Seed Weevil: Ensure proper spacing and apply sulfur sprays.

Interactive Checklist

  • Removed fallen fruits and leaves
  • Pruned dead/diseased branches
  • Applied organic pesticide
